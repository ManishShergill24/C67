He sees Me.
    Charlie dropped to her hands and knees.
She was wedged behind a row of arcade games,cramped in the crawl space
betweem the consoles and the wall, tangled electrical cords and useless
plugs strewn beneath her. SHe was cornered; the only way out was past the
thing, and she wasn't fast enough to make it. She could see him stalking 
back and forth, catching flickers of movement through gaps between the 
games. There was scarcely enough room to move, but she tried to crawl backward.
